About Me

My name is Jeremy Koeleman but online I often go by UnofficalTranslator. I am a narrative designer with a love for detective stories. I have been studying Creative Media & Game Technologies at Hanze university since 2022.

I started writing stories when I was 9 years old. I found my love for narrative design in DnD when I was 16, as I started to play in a world where anything could change and every choice had an effect. Now I am a Dungeon Master and narrative designer trying to spread the joy that I felt when I first fell in love with the craft.
